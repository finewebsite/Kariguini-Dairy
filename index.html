About Us

Karigu-ini Dairy Self Help Group, located in Karigu-ini village in Nyeri County, was established in 2005 with a mission to empower local dairy farmers.

With over 70 dedicated members, we provide a reliable platform where farmers can sell their milk at fair and competitive prices while ensuring consistent supply to local consumers and dairy companies.

We are committed to improving livelihoods, supporting sustainable farming, and building a strong dairy community.
